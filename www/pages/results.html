Results

The following videos show the results of our implementation of the CC algorithm within an RTS environment.

Scalar discomfort field

In the video below you can see an early implementation with a scalar discomfort field. As the groups start moving, they immediately form a "line". This is caused because the individual units in the groups try to avoid eachothers discomfort zones which are scalar values at this point.

Vector discomfort field

In this video we can see the top left facing bottom right. The "weaving lanes" formed here have a higher density this time, the discomfort field is vector based and no discomfort is "felt" when approaching from the right direction. That is, when approaching group-direction vector from an accute angle. Ensuring more coherency between the units of a group.

Weaving Lanes

The units in this video from both groups form nice "weaving lanes" in order to avoid each other.
